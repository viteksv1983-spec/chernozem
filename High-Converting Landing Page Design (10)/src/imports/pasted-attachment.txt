You are a senior product designer and visual designer working inside Figma.

Your task is to improve the visual consistency of the landing page by selecting and styling truck images used in the delivery options section.

The page sells black soil (chernozem) delivery in Kyiv and already has cards for different truck types:

• ZIL
• KAMAZ
• MAZ
• VOLVO

Your job is to introduce realistic truck images while preserving the existing card layout.

Important rules:

Do NOT change the layout grid.
Do NOT change card sizes significantly.
Do NOT redesign the section.
Only enhance it visually.

---

STEP 1 — SELECT APPROPRIATE TRUCK IMAGES

Choose realistic dump truck images for each card.

Truck mapping:

ZIL — small dump truck (older compact truck)
KAMAZ — medium dump truck (most common)
MAZ — large dump truck
VOLVO — heavy construction dump truck

Image requirements:

• realistic dump truck
• 3/4 front angle preferred
• high resolution
• neutral background or construction environment
• truck should be clearly visible
• no text or watermarks

---

STEP 2 — CREATE A UNIFIED IMAGE STYLE

All truck images must have a consistent visual style.

Apply the same treatment to each image:

• similar crop
• consistent height
• similar lighting feel
• same border radius
• same shadow style

Image styling:

• height around 160–200px
• object-fit: cover
• border-radius consistent with cards
• subtle shadow
• slight contrast increase if needed

---

STEP 3 — VISUAL HARMONY WITH THE WEBSITE

The landing page has an earthy aesthetic:

• dark soil textures
• green eco accents
• natural palette

Ensure truck images do not look too bright or saturated.

Apply subtle adjustments if needed:

• reduce saturation slightly
• slightly warm color temperature
• increase contrast very lightly

Goal: make the images blend naturally with the soil-themed design.

---

STEP 4 — CARD STRUCTURE

Inside each truck card the structure must remain:

[truck image]

truck name

capacity info

price range

short description

CTA button

Do not move these elements.

---

STEP 5 — VISUAL BALANCE

Ensure:

• all truck cards remain equal height
• images align horizontally
• spacing between cards stays consistent

Images must enhance clarity but not dominate the card.

---

FINAL GOAL

The truck section should feel:

• professional
• trustworthy
• visually consistent
• easy to understand

It should look like a polished logistics landing page designed by a professional studio.
